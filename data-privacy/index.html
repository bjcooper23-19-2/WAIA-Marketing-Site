What WAIA records

Records that support adoption visibility.

WAIA records product activity needed to support learning, guidance, acknowledgement, evidence and follow-up. It does not imply capture of every prompt or all workplace AI activity.

Learner and rollout records

Learner invitations, learner progress, completion records and reminders.

Guidance and evidence

Guidance acknowledgement, baseline responses and application evidence.

Administration

Admin activity relevant to product operation, reporting and support.

AI and customer data

How AI-related functionality is handled.

WAIA may include AI-related learning content, frameworks, examples and practical learning materials. Unless a feature clearly says otherwise, WAIA is not designed to submit Customer Personal Data to AI model providers for analysis or model training. Nineteen Point Two Ltd does not intentionally use Customer Personal Data to train AI models. If future WAIA features materially change how Customer Personal Data is processed by AI systems, the relevant customer documentation, product notices, privacy information, subprocessor list and contractual terms should be updated as required.

Customer responsibility

WAIA supports governance. It does not own it for the customer.

Customers remain responsible for their AI policies, legal and regulatory interpretation, lawful basis for employee data, privacy notices, employment decisions, tool approval, risk assessment, governance and enforcement.
