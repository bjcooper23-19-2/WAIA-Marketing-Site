Scope boundaries

What sits outside standard scope.

WAIA gives organisations a practical adoption layer. It doesn't bundle every advisory or integration need into the core licence.

Outside the core licence

  • Bespoke policy drafting.
  • Custom learning content.
  • Managed delivery or programme ownership.
  • System integrations.
  • Unlimited advisory time.

Commercial FAQ

Short answers before the first call.

What does eligible population mean?

It means the agreed group of people eligible to use WAIA, not the number who log in during a particular month.

Can we start with a smaller group?

Yes. The starting scope should match the organisation, sponsor and first workplace AI adoption priority.

Is Guided Adoption required?

No. It's available where extra interpretation and rollout support would be useful, but it isn't mandatory.

Does WAIA replace legal or compliance advice?

No. WAIA supports practical adoption evidence and guidance, but it is not legal advice, regulatory assurance or a GRC platform.
