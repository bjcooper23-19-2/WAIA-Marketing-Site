# WAIA Brand Pack

## Approved direction

**Route A: Open Wayfinder**

**Brand idea:** Make the next move visible.

The Open Wayfinder is a custom, open W formed from guided paths. It should feel calm, clear, practical, credible, human and operational. It must not feel futuristic, surveillance-led, cyber-security led or like generic AI software.

## Status

This folder is the working source of truth for the WAIA identity and shared visual system.

The assets in `brand/assets/` are production-ready web SVGs for the marketing-site pilot. The geometry is deliberately simple, rounded and robust at small sizes. The lockups use Inter, which is already the WAIA interface typeface.

Do not replace the live site identity until the marketing-site implementation branch has passed visual and accessibility QA.

## Core rules

1. Use operating navy for the product shell, navigation and high-level product context.
2. Use clarity white for instruction, decision content, expected action and important explanation.
3. Use deep teal for readable action and emphasis on light surfaces.
4. Use bright cyan as illumination on dark surfaces, not as body text on pale backgrounds.
5. Keep the mark open. Do not add a box, shield, eye, brain, robot, circuit pattern, sparkle or literal arrowhead.
6. Keep the standalone mark legible at 20px and above. Use the compact lockup when vertical space is limited.
7. WAIA remains the primary product identity. Nineteen Point Two appears only as the legal operator or secondary endorsement.

## Product alignment

The marketing site is the first controlled implementation. Once approved, the same mark, tokens and clarity-panel pattern should be applied to the WAIA platform in a separate product branch.
